1)Remove the cap from a clean plastic bottle.
Use scissors to carefully poke some small drainage holes in the base of the bottle.

2)Use your scissors to make two level slits on opposite sides of the bottle,
near to the base.

3) Push a stick through the holes. There should be around 5 centimetres
of stick left outside the bottle on each side

4) Slightly above each perch, use the scissors to
cut a feeding hole in the shape of an arch.
